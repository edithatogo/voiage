\section{Limitations}

The repository evidence establishes reproducible software behavior for tested
fixtures and supported environments; it does not establish fitness for every
decision context or replace domain validation. Users remain responsible for
model structure, input provenance, distributional assumptions, willingness-to-
pay thresholds, and interpretation. Optional accelerator and experimental
methods have narrower evidence boundaries than the stable Rust-backed core.

Authorship, affiliation, ORCID, funding, conflicts of interest, research-impact
evidence, arXiv category, license, and endorsement remain subject to final
human confirmation. A successful automated build does not imply arXiv upload,
moderation, announcement, journal submission, review, or acceptance.
