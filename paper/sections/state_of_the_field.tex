\section{State of the field}

VOI analysis is well established in decision analysis and health
economics~\cite{bradley1993expected,claxton1999value,adams2020value}. The R
ecosystem includes mature packages for particular health-economic workflows,
including Bayesian cost-effectiveness analysis and EVSI methods.
\texttt{voiage} complements rather than replaces those packages by providing a
Python-first, cross-domain interface and by making its execution contract
portable to Rust, R, and Julia. The project emphasizes shared fixtures and
cross-language conformance instead of maintaining a separate numerical policy
implementation in each binding. Its extension policy also separates stable
methods from optional and experimental research surfaces.
