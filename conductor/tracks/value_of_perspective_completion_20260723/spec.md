# Track Specification: Value Of Perspective Completion

## Overview

Complete directional and uncertain-perspective decision analysis under the
shared VOP C01/C02 contracts.

## Requirements

1. Implement directional and ordered pairwise EVoP/regret, perspective optima
   and CEAC/CEAF, the Perspective Acceptability Frontier, mixture switch
   regions, perfect/partial/sample perspective information, and joint
   parameter-perspective decompositions.
2. Retain consensus, maximin, minimax-regret, robust, and Pareto summaries.
3. Validate raw-outcome transformations for payer, societal, patient, provider,
   regulator, equity-weighted, and custom perspectives.
4. Keep perspective distinct from preference, heterogeneity, equity, and
   deterministic scenarios.

## Compatibility and failure policy

Extend the versioned shared contract additively. Preserve directional
asymmetry, reference perspective, weights, and tie policy. Incomparable
surfaces, invalid priors, ambiguous attribution, and double-counted uncertainty
fail closed.

## Acceptance criteria

Every named perspective estimand is formal, fixture-backed, cross-language
conformant, and honestly matured; synthetic and rights-cleared public examples
pass.

## External gates

C01/C02 shared-contract approval and real-data perspective attribution.
