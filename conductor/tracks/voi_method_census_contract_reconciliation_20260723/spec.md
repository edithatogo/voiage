# Track Specification: VOI/VOP Method Census And Contract Reconciliation

## Overview

Build the canonical, literature-derived registry of VOI, Value of Perspective,
and related methods before the v1.1 contract freeze.

## Requirements

1. Search decision analysis, health economics, Bayesian design, statistics,
   machine learning, operations research, environmental science, engineering,
   and information economics with recorded queries, dates, sources, and
   inclusion decisions.
2. Distinguish estimands, estimators, applications, visualizations, and aliases.
3. Record identifier, equation, assumptions, inputs, outputs, units, scaling,
   tie and RNG policy, maturity, implementations, bindings, fixtures,
   citations, performance, limitations, and promotion gates.
4. Reconcile `roadmap.md`, documentation, exports, schemas, runtime, and
   frontier-governance claims.
5. Generate capabilities and public comparison material from the registry.

## Compatibility, risk, and gates

Registry changes are versioned and additive; aliases and staged deprecations
preserve v1 clients. Citation error, duplicate terminology, inaccessible
sources, and maturity inflation fail closed. SourceRight and human scientific
review are required before publication claims.

## Acceptance criteria

Every current method has an evidence-backed disposition; stable methods have
formal definitions and validation; generated surfaces are deterministic; no
narrative/runtime maturity conflict remains.

## Out of scope

Automatic acceptance of a literature claim or automatic maturity promotion.
