# C15 specification: Operational Assurance Excellence

## Overview

Operationalize C14 without weakening fail-closed or human-approval boundaries.
VOIAGE independently consumes the staged bundle and proves multi-version
compatibility, changed-line and critical coverage, mutation-cohort integrity,
cross-environment reproducibility, statistically controlled performance,
collector-level telemetry privacy, and expanded scientific references.

## Functional requirements

1. Verify current, N-1, and intentionally incompatible bundle migrations without
   importing VOP runtime code.
2. Enforce critical-module and changed-line branch coverage with retained JSON.
3. Identify mutation cohorts by source/configuration digest and retain score,
   absolute debt, density, universe changes, and promotion provenance.
4. Compare normalized Linux and Windows distributions by exact digest.
5. Record runner fingerprints, repeated samples, confidence intervals, and
   deterministic performance decisions.
6. Export representative telemetry through an ephemeral collector and verify
   correlation plus secret absence in the exported payload.
7. Validate boundary, near-tie, tail, and higher-dimensional reference cases
   across available backends and bindings.

## Acceptance criteria

- All repository-owned local and exact-head hosted gates pass.
- Independent review finds no remaining Critical, High, or Medium implementation
  defect.
- Merge, governance-baseline approval, issue closure, and publication remain
  explicit human gates.

## Out of scope

Autonomous merge, trusted-baseline approval, signing, public release, issue
closure, Project mutation beyond requested synchronization, and paid hardware.
