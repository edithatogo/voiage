---
title: Portfolio Optimization
description: Research Portfolio Value of Information
---

Portfolio VOI evaluates combinations of candidate studies under a shared budget or capacity constraint. Rather than assessing studies in isolation, this method identifies the optimal research portfolio that maximises the total expected value of information given resource limitations.
