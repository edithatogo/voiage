---
title: Regulatory and market-access VOI
description: Estimate the value of information that changes approval, reimbursement, label, price, or coverage decisions.
---

The fixture-backed `value_of_regulatory_market_access` method models approval
and reimbursement probabilities across approved, restricted-label,
coverage-with-evidence, and rejected scenarios. It reports regulatory and payer
uncertainty, access delay, evidence-package cost, label expansion, and
price-threshold value.

Calibrated regulatory/payer data, cross-language parity, and mature/stable
governance approval remain required before promotion.

Use `voiage generate-config regulatory-market-access` to create a starting JSON
payload, then run `voiage calculate-regulatory-market-access INPUT.json`.
