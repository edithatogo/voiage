---
title: Sequential VOI
description: Sequential Decision Value of Information
---

Sequential VOI studies how the value of information changes when decisions can be delayed while additional evidence accumulates over multiple stages. This family addresses multi-period decision problems where early information may inform later choices and where the timing of evidence collection matters.
