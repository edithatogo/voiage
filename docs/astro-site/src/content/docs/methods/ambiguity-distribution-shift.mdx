---
title: Ambiguity and distribution-shift VOI
description: Fixture-backed scenario-shift information value
---

`value_of_ambiguity_distribution_shift` evaluates source-sample reweightings
that represent plausible target populations or drift scenarios. It reports
radius-penalized robust net benefit, scenario regret, shift sensitivity, and
the expected value of resolving the scenario.

This method is experimental and distinct from model validation and causal
transportability. Stable promotion requires Rust and retained-binding parity,
licensed source/target evidence, and mature/stable approval.
