---
title: Adaptive Trials
description: Adaptive Trial Expected Value of Sample Information
---

Adaptive trial EVSI estimates the value of information from clinical trials that incorporate interim analyses and potential design modifications. This method accounts for the ability to stop early, adjust sample sizes, or modify treatment arms as evidence accumulates during the study.
