---
title: Observational VOI
description: Value of Information from Observational Data
---

Observational VOI covers the value of improving evidence that comes from non-randomised or real-world data sources, including cohort studies, registries, and administrative databases. This method accounts for the additional uncertainty introduced by confounding, selection bias, and measurement error typical of observational designs.
