---
title: Dominance
description: Dominance Analysis in Cost-Effectiveness
---

Dominance analysis identifies strategies that are strictly dominated (higher cost and lower effectiveness than an alternative) or weakly dominated (subject to elimination by extended dominance rules). This analysis simplifies the decision space by removing inferior strategies before applying VOI methods.
