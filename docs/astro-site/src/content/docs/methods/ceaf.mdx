---
title: CEAF
description: Cost-Effectiveness Acceptability Frontier
---

The Cost-Effectiveness Acceptability Frontier identifies the strategy with the highest expected net benefit across all willingness-to-pay thresholds. Unlike the CEAC, which can show multiple strategies with non-zero probability of being cost-effective, the CEAF resolves ambiguity by selecting the single optimal strategy at each threshold.
