---
title: VOI Frontier and Method Maturity
description: Frontier methods, evidence status, and promotion boundaries
---

The VOI frontier catalogue records methods that extend the stable Rust-backed
kernel. A method may be useful and documented without being a v1 stable
guarantee.

## Maturity labels

- **Stable** methods are covered by the versioned stable API and compatibility
  policy.
- **Fixture-backed** methods have deterministic contract fixtures but still need
  broader evidence before promotion.
- **Experimental** methods are isolated under [`voiage.experimental`](/api-reference/)
  or an explicitly labelled method surface and may change without stable API
  guarantees.

Promotion requires contract validation, provenance, reproducibility, security
review, and—where relevant—cross-language parity and licensed evidence. See the
[migration guide](/user-guide/migration-guide/) before depending on a
fixture-backed or experimental method in production.
