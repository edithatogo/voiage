## Interpretation and exclusions

- `native` and `equivalent` require VOIAGE-owned execution; no competing VOI
  package may be required at runtime.
- `adapter` is migration or interchange support, not delegated numerical authority.
- `not-reproducible` is used for behavior that cannot currently be independently
  observed or pinned, especially proprietary and inactive web tools.
- General model construction, posterior inference, and generic acquisition
  optimization remain delegated when they are not themselves VOI estimands.
- EIG, BALD, entropy search, and similar scores are not called economic VOI unless
  a downstream decision and utility or loss are explicit.

The source registry, recorded searches, versions, licenses, and limitations are in
[`specs/software-landscape/registry.json`](https://github.com/edithatogo/voiage/tree/main/specs/software-landscape).
