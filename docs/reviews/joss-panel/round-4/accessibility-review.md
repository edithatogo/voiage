## Deductions and recommendations

- Replace broad software jargon where direct wording is available.
- Avoid absolute comparisons with the wider field.
- Use lower-case “normal” in ordinary prose.
- Give the units with every reported VOI estimate.
- Replace “currency-equivalent scale” with a direct definition of value units.
- Describe the figure in terms of positive incremental net benefit, estimated
  EVPPI, and value realisation.
- Shorten axis labels and use the same terms in prose and figures.
- Acknowledge that R and Julia installation is less direct than Python.
- Keep the title under review because “Implementation” may suggest a fuller
  implementation model than the paper demonstrates.
